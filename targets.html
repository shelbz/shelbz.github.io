Targets

Who has the power to give you what you want? **Remember, a target is always a person or persons.


Remember, your group are the ones building the world you want -- you just need to get these folks to cooperate.


Is there a secondary target, or someone who has the power over your primary target? What power do you have over them?

If you have a secondary target, you may need to reevaluate/edit your tactics or goals.
